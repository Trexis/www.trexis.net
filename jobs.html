CONSULTANT/DEVELOPER POSITION



The team

Trexis Expert Services is a small team of highly motivated specialists with the specific skills and experience required to make sure that the software implementation at our customer’s location goes as smoothly as possible. The team also provides product training and implementation support services to all of our customers. Every team member must be able to think and react quickly to find a solution that works for everyone. They have the professional and personal skills required to develop, teach, support and lead teams towards successfully delivering an enterprise solution and achieving their goals.


The goal

As a hands-on back-end consultant, you will be responsible for developing (web-based) applications into the enterprise ICT environments of our international clients. During our pilot and implementation projects you will be part of a highly skilled multidisciplinary team. You will work closely with clients, architects and front-end consultants to define the integration with the client’s back-end systems; determine implementation details; and realise those implementations. You will also be responsible for integrating security layers, exposing relevant business data and, when necessary, develop custom extensions to third party applications.


Your profile

We are looking for motivated candidates who meet the following criteria:

  • Have good presentation skills;
  • Experience in a consultancy role;
  • Excellent communication skills in English;
  • Extreme flexibility in terms of work location and a willingness to travel abroad frequently, sometimes for long periods of time.

Technically we are looking for:

  • A minimum of two years’ development experience within Web/Enterprise projects;
  • Understanding Design patterns and N-Tiers architectures.
  • Experience with the following would be advantageous:
    • Providing sizing and scalability recommendations and solving connectivity and deployment issues;
    • Deploying to application servers in enterprise environments;
    • Experienced with using Agile development methodologies.;
    • Java EE, Maven;
    • Application servers such as Jetty, Tomcat, JBoss, Websphere;
    • Spring and other Java frameworks/open source libraries;
    • Spring Security and SSO solutions;
    • SOA and Web Services (REST/SOAP);
    • Database applications such as Oracle, MySql, DB2 as well as with SQL, JDBC and ORM tools;
    • Web/Enterprise Content Management Systems.

The offer

  • Market-related salary (depending on experience);
  • 21 holidays (based upon a 40-hour work week);
  • Compensation for home away days (days travelled international);
