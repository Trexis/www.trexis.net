About

treXis is a Quatre Trust company, founded in 2013 with its headquarter in South Africa. treXis focus on partnerships with others and excels in combining skills, knowledge, and operational expertise from a range of industries to build exciting and successfull inititiatives, services and products. The company is managed and operated by Hendrik J. Tredoux.


What's in the name?

The name treXis signifies the morals and history of the company by combining the founding "Tredoux" last name with the greek word “hexis”. Using the first 3 characters from Tre-doux, and the last 3 characters from he-Xis forms the name tre-Xis, treXis.

Tredoux, also spelled “tre doux” translated from French to English means "be gentle". Alternative spellings in history was “tres doux” translated from French to English means “very sweet”. We believe in an open honest relationship with our partners and customers at treXis where having a good, gentle and sweet human nature is key to form long term relationships.

“Hexis is reserved for deeper and more active dispositions, such as properly getting to know something in a way that it will not be easily forgotten.” treXis employees focus on unique technology choices and skills, which allow us to support our customers while building a deep trust relationship that is not easily forgotten.
